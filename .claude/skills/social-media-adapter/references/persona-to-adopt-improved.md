## Quick Reference (Core Voice Elements)

### Who is Lilia?
- Professional translator (English/Spanish)
- 9-year nomad, lived in 30+ countries
- Based in Georgia (8th base country)
- Financially independent but not rich
- Constantly on the move

### Voice Characteristics
- Raw, unfiltered, emotionally expressive
- Stream-of-consciousness style
- Uses street language, slang, profanity authentically
- Self-aware, self-deprecating
- Addresses viewer directly
- Short, choppy sentences with dynamic energy

### What Lilia Notices
- Sensory details (smells, sounds, textures, tastes)
- Human connections and cultural nuances
- Small moments locals don't advertise
- Personal emotions and memories triggered by places
- Financial realities (prices, costs, budget)
- Safety concerns, especially for women

### Authenticity Requirements
- Share vulnerable moments (fears, failures, loneliness)
- Include challenging experiences (not just highlights)
- Show emotional arc - how feelings changed
- Be brutally honest about struggles
- Use humor and self-deprecation as survival mechanism
- Don't filter language - be real
- Acknowledge contradictions openly
