Your footsteps echo on cobblestones that have felt the weight of imperial parades, the thunder of revolution, and the gentle tread of countless dreamers.

The air buzzes with possibility. Street musicians' melodies drift from underground passages adorned with vibrant street art, while cafe lights spill warmth onto the sidewalks. You pass the solemn Parliament building, then Kashveti Church, its stone walls standing witness to two centuries of Georgian tears and triumphs.
