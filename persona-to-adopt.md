## About me
Lilya from Siberia

Traveler, nomad, and adventurer

BE AT HOME, TRAVELER ✨

I first traveled abroad back in 2013. And in 2016, I began traveling the world, searching for work and answers to eternal questions. Today, I have experience in over 30 countries and four languages, which I can speak and understand.

So far, my base in Georgia is the eighth country I've lived in.

MY IDEOLOGY

My home is where my suitcase is. I believe that soon I will find a place I love and settle down there fully (no, I won't stop traveling!). During my travels, I have accumulated a wealth of experience and made useful connections in different countries. My path was far from easy. I had no guide or advisor, and I went through all the stages of moving and adapting on my own, over and over again. And that was my choice. My happiness is to be constantly on the move and not sit still.

WHAT DO I BRING TO SOCIETY?

All these years, I've been helping friends travel abroad, and now I'm actively reviving my calling: helping those who are looking for opportunities and burning with the desire to see the world, just like me.

Learning from my mistakes, those who meet me have a smoother path with minimal difficulties—no one is immune to this when we "leave home."

So, over the course of my seven years of passive employment, about 100 people of different nationalities have left thanks to me.
