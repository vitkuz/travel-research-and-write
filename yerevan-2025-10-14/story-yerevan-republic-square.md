# Republic Square: Where Yerevan's Heart Beats

You step into Republic Square as dusk settles over Yerevan, and the transformation begins. The pink and yellow tuff buildings encircling you glow like embers in the fading light, their neoclassical facades whispering tales of Alexander Tamanian's 1924 vision. Beneath your feet, the stone pattern mimics a traditional Armenian rug, each step connecting you to centuries of craftsmanship.
